About Us

Our History

Akshat Lolaki, the company's founder, opened the first Tasty Treat Factory restaurant in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, in 2001. The restaurant established the future chain's pattern of featuring an eclectic menu, large portions, and signature cheesecakes.In 2020, Fortune magazine ranked The Tasty Treat Factory at number 1 on their Fortune List of the Top 100 Companies to Work for.

The Factory traces its humble beginnings to The Desi Treat, a successful dhaba started by our founder, Mr. Akshat Lolaki, that featured for the first time on the India's best road side eateries.

Corporate Leadership

Akshat Lolaki Chief Gourmet Officer

With great foresight and intuition, Our CEO Akshat Lolaki decided to open a restaurant to showcase his grand mother’s selection of home made dessert recipes. Somehow he just knew that guests would enjoy eating out place with an extensive dessert menu. It was 2000 and he opened the first Tasty Treat Factory in Dehradun, UK. The restaurant was an immediate success and today there are more than 100 Tasty Treat Factory restaurants around India that share the Lolaki's commitment to healthy food, quality, innovation and hard work.

Akshay Lolaki Master Chef

Akshay was born in New Delhi to a Pahari family. From a very early age, Akshay took a liking to cooking and pursued it to become one of the most well-known chefs in India. Akshay has an in-depth understanding of regional Indian cuisine, having learnt it from the best in the industry. His motto innovative Indian cuisine that, at par with the best culinary standards>.
